7.1 Megapixels is an ever-growing series shot on a Canon Powershot SD800 that has, as the title of the series says, 7.1 megapixels. I call this camera my 'baby cam'. This series is dedicated to the beauty of the moment, the fun of photography, and overall just a way for me to showcase some moments I really and truly adore. This series is a look into my experiences through life as I photograph all the things I love with a camera that lends to nostalgia. I hope others can enjoy these moments of life as I do.
