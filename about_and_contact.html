About & Contact

Leela Pereira is a people person - and photographer! - who splits her time between the GTA and Perth County. Focusing on portraiture, fashion, and beauty, she creates imagery that resonates with her sitters. An appreciation for cinematography and narrative drives her to create imagery that feels like real moments and familiar experiences. She tends to lean into the unpredictable, searching for ways to make something new and unique to her. Leela also styles a lot of her own sets and models; she is passionate about fashion and about creating the right setting to fit both the model and the vision.
